# Global Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is an Excel-based data analysis dashboard created to understand global sales performance across different regions, countries, item types, order priorities, and sales channels.

The dataset contains 5,000 orders and was analyzed to find key business insights such as total revenue, total profit, profit margin, top-performing countries, regional revenue contribution, online vs offline sales split, and average shipping time.

## Dashboard Highlights

- Total Revenue: 6.63B
- Total Profit: 1.96B
- Profit Margin: 29.60%
- Total Orders: 5,000
- Revenue and Profit Trend Analysis
- Region-wise Revenue Analysis
- Top 10 Countries by Profit
- Profit by Item Type
- Online vs Offline Sales Split
- Average Shipping Days by Region
- Revenue by Order Priority
- Global Revenue Distribution Map

## Key Insights

- Total revenue reached 6.63B with 1.96B profit, giving an overall profit margin of 29.6%.
- Revenue and profit were strongest around 2015, while 2017 appears lower because data is available only till July.
- Sub-Saharan Africa and Europe are the leading regions in revenue contribution.
- Online and offline sales are almost balanced, showing that both channels are important.
- Cosmetics, Household, and Office Supplies are the major profit-driving item types.
- Average shipping time is around 25 days, so region-wise shipping performance can be improved.
- Top 10 countries contribute strongly to total profit, making them key markets for business growth.
